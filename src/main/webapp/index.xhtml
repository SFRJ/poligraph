Plolygraph V1 - A happiness monitor for the team

Welcome to Polygraph V1,
This application monitors the mood of team.
Every working day at 5.00pm the polygraph will ask to each team member, How was your day?
The answers will be used to determine the overall mood of the team across time

Why team mood monitor is good?

  • Temperature reading(Does the team need a retrospective?)
  • Show how key events in the office can affect team members mood
  • Anticipate job quit/resignation
  • Encorage communication about issues
  • Show to others what is like to work in the team
  • Because we believe in better...
